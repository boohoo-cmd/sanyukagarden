Welcome to Sanyuka

Where Kampala comes to slow down, gather beneath old trees, and eat food grown by the hands of local farmers.

The Setting

A garden, not a dining room

Sanyuka sits within the historic gardens of Mengo Palace — the seat of the Buganda Kingdom. Mature trees, open air, and dappled light make every meal feel like an escape from the city just beyond the gate.

Come for a quiet lunch, a long dinner, or a celebration that spills into the evening. The garden holds them all.

Royal Heritage

Rooted in the Buganda Kingdom

To dine here is to sit on ground at the heart of one of Africa's oldest kingdoms. We honour that legacy — in our setting, our local sourcing, and the traditional dishes we keep alive.
